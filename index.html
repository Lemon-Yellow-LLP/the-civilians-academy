About us

Elevating the Civil
Services Outlook

Civil Services Examinations are one of the most prestigious and competitive opportunities in our country. The Civilians Academy is a vision-driven organization whose singular aim is to support and empower the future nation-builders of the country. Under the valuable guidance of Dr. Dnyaneshwar Mulay, our commitment is to build an equitable society, with you.

Vision

To bring the necessary change in students' approach towards Civil Services Examinations from a young age.

To create and contribute towards achieving a sustainable, sensitive, responsive, and responsible bureaucracy.

Work towards attaining a positive societal change through education and contribute towards the establishment of an equitable society.

Mission

To inculcate an understanding of the Civil Services Examination pattern from the early days of schooling under our dynamic “Young IAS” program.

To increase the percentage of candidates from among deprived sections of the society in the Civil Services.

To provide quality notes and study material (Marathi & English) and to impart training through quality faculty & teaching.

The Expert

Dr. Dnyaneshwar Mulay

Dr. Dyneshwar Mulay is known as the “Passport Man of India” for his exceptional work as a diplomat. Under his leadership, he took the country’s passport service to people. His effort to introduce passport office to every Lok Sabha constituency in the country gave rise to 410 new passport offices in India.

Member, National Human Right Commission
Former Secreatry, Ministry of External Affairs
Founder of Movement of Positivity
A prolific author of over 15 books, translated across languages
